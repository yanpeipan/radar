### Phase 19: article view 命令增强

**Goal**: 增强 `feedship article view` 命令，支持 --url/--id/--json 参数，Trafilatura 最佳实践提取内容
**Depends on**: None
**Plans**: 1 plan

Plans:

- [x] 19-01: article view --url/--id/--json 实现 (VIEW-01~04)

**Details:**

**Requirements:** VIEW-01, VIEW-02, VIEW-03, VIEW-04

**Success Criteria** (what must be TRUE):
1. `feedship article view --url <URL>` fetches URL, extracts content with Trafilatura, returns Markdown, does not store to DB
2. `feedship article view --id <article_id>` looks up article in DB, fetches its link, extracts content, updates DB content field, returns Markdown
3. `feedship article view --json` outputs JSON with url/title/content/extracted_at fields
4. --url and --id are mutually exclusive, using both together exits with error
5. Trafilatura uses output_format=markdown, include_images=False, include_tables=True
